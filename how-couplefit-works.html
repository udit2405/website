The Roadmap

Your Transformation Week by Week

Every week of your programme has a defined purpose. Here's how a 20-week couple transformation unfolds — from the day you both join to the morning you walk into your wedding together.

Weeks 1–2  ·  Couple Foundation Phase

Joint Assessment, Calibration & Movement Baseline

We establish individual baselines for both partners — body composition, movement quality, and metabolic calibration for each. Both diet plans are set at the right calorie levels. Training begins with controlled volume to build the movement patterns that will support each partner's specific transformation phase. The shared kitchen nutrition framework is introduced. Both of you start together, from the same moment.

Weeks 3–8  ·  Transformation Phase

Individual Fat Loss & Toning — Coordinated as a Couple

The primary transformation block runs in parallel for both partners — each on an individually calibrated fat-loss and toning protocol, both following the same shared meal framework. You train separately if needed, but the results are building together. Weekly check-ins review both partners simultaneously. You'll notice changes in each other before you notice them in the mirror.

Weeks 9–16  ·  Couple Recomposition Phase

Sculpt the Physiques That Make the Outfits Extraordinary

Body fat is dropping for both partners. Now the programme targets what makes a couple look extraordinary side by side in photographs — shoulder width and V-taper for the sherwani or suit, bridal posture, toned arms, and elegance for the lehenga. Both partners' strength and confidence is rising each week. The photographs from this period look different. Both of you can feel it.

Week 16  ·  Final Outfit Fittings — Both Partners

Lock In Both Physiques — Get the Outfits Stitched to These Bodies

Strategic timing for both partners. By week 16, each physique is at or near its target shape — and this is when we recommend both partners do their final sherwani, suit, or lehenga fitting. The outfits are stitched to the bodies built during the programme, not the bodies from the day you signed up. This single change in timing makes a visible difference in every photograph.

Weeks 17–20  ·  Couple Peak Protocol

Both of You — at Your Best — on the Same Morning.

Individual peak protocols for each partner, timed in parallel. Strategic nutrition, water management, and recovery optimisation running simultaneously for both of you. On the morning of your wedding, both partners follow their precise protocols. You wake up — side by side — looking the best either of you have looked throughout the entire preparation. Every photograph from that day will show exactly what you built, together.
