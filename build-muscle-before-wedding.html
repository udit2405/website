Common Mistakes When Trying to Build Muscle Fast

Bulking season nahi hai bhai — this is wedding season.

  • Eating too little — muscles need fuel to grow
  • Only doing isolation exercises (curls, pushdowns)
  • Not training with enough intensity
  • Skipping workouts and being inconsistent
  • Ignoring sleep — where most recovery happens

Avoid these and you’re already ahead of most grooms.

The Muscle-Building Workout Plan for Grooms

This is a hypertrophy-focused split for maximum visual impact:

  • Day 1 — Chest & Shoulders: Bench press, incline press, lateral raises, shoulder press, cable flyes
  • Day 2 — Back: Deadlifts, pull-ups, rows, lat pulldown, face pulls
  • Day 3 — Arms & Core: Curls, tricep dips, pushdowns, plank variations
  • Day 4 — Legs: Squats, Romanian deadlift, leg press, lunges
  • Day 5 — Full Body + Cardio: Compound circuit + 20 min cardio

Use progressive overload — increase weight or reps every 1–2 weeks.

Diet to Build Muscle Before Wedding

Protein is king.

  • Protein: 1.8–2.2g per kg bodyweight — eggs, chicken, paneer, dal, whey
  • Calories: Slight surplus (200–300) for muscle gain
  • Carbs: Oats, rice, fruits — fuel your workouts
  • Fats: Ghee, nuts, olive oil — support hormones
  • Eat every 3–4 hours

This is what actually builds muscle — not random diets.
