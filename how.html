The Roadmap

Your Transformation Week by Week

Every week of your programme has a defined purpose. Here's how a 20-week transformation unfolds — from the day you join to the morning of your wedding.

Weeks 1–2  ·  Foundation Phase

Assessment, Calibration & Movement Quality

We establish your true baseline — body composition measurements, movement screening, and metabolic calibration. Your diet is set at the right calorie level. Training begins with controlled volume to build the movement patterns that will support heavy progressive overload in the coming phases. No guesswork, no generic start.

Weeks 3–8  ·  Fat Loss Phase

Strategic Calorie Deficit + Muscle Preservation

The primary fat-loss block. Training volume is high, intensity is progressive, and diet is managed in a controlled deficit that maximises fat loss while protecting the muscle you're simultaneously building. Most clients notice a visible waist reduction of 5–8 cm in this phase alone. Weekly check-ins ensure the plan adapts to your real-world response.

Weeks 9–16  ·  Recomposition Phase

Build the Suit-Fit Physique — Shoulders, Chest, Arms

Body fat is dropping. Now we aggressively build the muscle that makes a suit look extraordinary — shoulders, upper chest, upper back, and arms. This is the phase that creates the V-taper that turns a good-fitting suit into one that looks custom-designed for your body. Strength numbers rise each week. The mirror starts showing the difference.

Week 16  ·  Final Suit Fitting

Lock In the Physique — Get the Suit Tailored to This Body

Strategic timing. Your physique is now at or near its target shape. This is when we recommend your final suit or sherwani fitting — after the transformation, not before it. The suit is stitched to the body you built, not the one you started with.

Weeks 17–20  ·  Peak Protocol

Maximum Definition. Zero Bloat. Wedding Day Ready.

Strategic carbohydrate cycling, water management, and recovery optimisation. Training intensity reduces to protect muscle fullness. The morning of your wedding, you follow a precise eating and hydration protocol designed to make your muscles look full and sharp — and your waist as defined as it's ever been. This is the final chapter. And it looks exactly like you planned.
