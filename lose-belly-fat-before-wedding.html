How to Lose Belly Fat Before Wedding: 60-Day Plan

You're 2 months away from your wedding and that stubborn belly is still there.

This is not generic advice. This is a step-by-step plan built for Indian grooms who want real visible change in 60 days.

What’s Actually Possible in 60 Days?

  • Fat loss: 4–8 kg realistically
  • Visible belly reduction
  • Sharper face and jawline
  • Massive confidence boost

Why Belly Fat Is Stubborn for Indian Men

  • High-carb diet (roti, rice, sugar)
  • Late-night eating
  • Low protein intake
  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • High sodium snacks

The 60-Day Plan (Simple Breakdown)

Phase 1 (Days 1–20)

  • Cut maida and sugar
  • Drink 3.5L water daily
  • Eat dinner before 8:30 PM
  • Start basic workouts (squats, push-ups, walking)

Phase 2 (Days 21–45)

  • Increase protein (eggs, paneer, dal)
  • Reduce carbs
  • Add structured workouts (gym or home)
  • Stop packaged food completely

Phase 3 (Days 46–60)

  • Reduce salt
  • No alcohol
  • Focus on sleep
  • Daily steps: 8,000–10,000

Grooming Hacks to Look Leaner Instantly

  • Get a haircut that suits your face
  • Trim beard for jawline
  • Ice face in morning
  • Wear well-fitted clothes
  • Fix posture

Biggest Mistakes to Avoid

  • Crash dieting
  • Only doing abs exercises
  • Skipping breakfast
  • Not tracking progress
  • Starting too late
