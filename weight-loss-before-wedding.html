60–90 Day Weight Loss Plan

Workout

  • Strength training 4–5x/week
  • Cardio 20–30 mins
  • Focus on basics (pushups, squats, etc.)

Diet (Indian)

  • Breakfast → Eggs / paneer / oats
  • Lunch → Roti + sabzi + protein
  • Dinner → Light meal
  • Snacks → Fruits, nuts

Daily Habits

  • 8–10k steps
  • 7 hrs sleep
  • 2–3L water

Timeline Based on Wedding Date

90 days: Full transformation possible

60 days: Visible fat loss

30 days: Minor changes only

Common Mistakes

  • Crash dieting
  • Skipping meals
  • Only cardio
  • No consistency
