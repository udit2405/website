Most brides wait until a month out. By then there's no time for real change — only panic. A structured 60–90 day plan changes everything. Timing is everything.

Wedding planning stress is real — and it shows up in your body. Without proper nutrition and recovery, pre-wedding bloating is almost guaranteed. BrideFit accounts for all of it.

A vague goal of "losing weight" before the wedding isn't a plan. Without targeted toning, posture work, and a bridal-specific structure, results are unpredictable at best.
