60-Day Plan for Grooms

SECTION 01

The Mathematics of 60-Day Fat Loss

To lose fat aggressively but safely, you need to understand the numbers. One kilogram of body fat contains roughly 7,700 calories. To lose approximately 0.75kg of fat per week (the optimal rate to preserve muscle), you need a weekly deficit of about 5,000 calories.

The Dietary Cut

Calculate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). Subtract 500-700 calories from it. This ensures you are still eating enough to preserve muscle and brain function while forcing your body to tap into fat reserves.

The Activity Burn

Make up the remaining deficit through activity. A 45-minute heavy lifting session plus 10,000 daily steps easily burns an additional 400+ calories without starving yourself.

SECTION 02

Phase 1: The Aggressive Cut (Days 1–30)

The first 30 days are about stopping the influx of garbage, dropping initial water weight, and fixing your gut health to reduce bloating.

  • Liquid Calorie Ban: Immediately cut out all alcohol, sugary chai, sodas, and juices. This alone creates a massive caloric deficit for most Indian men without feeling hungry.
  • Double Your Protein: You must hit 1.8g to 2g of protein per kg of bodyweight. Incorporate eggs, paneer, chicken breast, and whey isolate daily. High protein keeps you full and preserves muscle in a calorie deficit.
  • Carbohydrate Timing: Do not eat large portions of rice or roti right before bed. Consume your carbohydrates in the meal immediately following your workout, when your muscles are primed to absorb them rather than store them as fat.

SECTION 03

Phase 2: Training & NEAT (Days 31–50)

To accelerate weight loss without losing your masculine frame, your training must be intense and purposeful in the second month.

❌ Mistake to Avoid

Do not spend 2 hours a day on the treadmill. Excessive cardio spikes cortisol (the stress hormone) and breaks down muscle tissue, leaving you looking "skinny-fat."

✓ The Groom Protocol

Focus heavily on Squats, Deadlifts, and Overhead Presses 4 days a week. These trigger massive testosterone responses and preserve muscle. For fat loss, focus on NEAT (10,000 steps daily). Walking burns pure fat without stressing your nervous system.

SECTION 04

Phase 3: The Peak Week (Days 51–60)

The final 10 days. This is when the stress of wedding planning usually peaks. The goal here is peaking your physique for the cameras and dropping any final water retention.

  • Sodium Manipulation: In the final 7 days, reduce your added salt intake slightly and increase your water intake to 4 liters a day. 48 hours before the wedding, drop your water intake slightly. This flushes out subcutaneous fluid, sharpening your jawline instantly.
  • Crush Cortisol with Sleep: Protect your 7-8 hours of sleep fiercely. High stress and low sleep elevate cortisol, which holds onto stubborn belly fat. Set a hard cut-off for wedding planning texts by 9:00 PM.
SECTION 05

Sample 60-Day Indian Diet Structure

You do not have to abandon Indian food to lose weight, but you do have to restructure the traditional plate.

Daily Macro Strategy
Rule 1
The 50% Veggie Plate

For lunch and dinner, half of your plate must be fibrous vegetables or salad. This fills your stomach for almost zero calories, preventing you from overeating the calorie-dense gravies and carbs.

Rule 2
Automate Breakfast

Decision fatigue will kill your diet. Eat the exact same high-protein breakfast every single day for 60 days (e.g., 4 egg omelette or a heavy whey protein smoothie). One less thing to think about.

Rule 3
The 8:00 PM Cut-off

Stop eating 3 hours before bed. Late-night digestion interferes with deep sleep and halts your overnight fat-burning window (when growth hormone naturally spikes).
