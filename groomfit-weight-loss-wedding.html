Weight Loss Before Wedding — 60 Day Plan

This is the exact roadmap to lose 4–8 kg before your wedding without crash dieting.

What’s Actually Possible in 60 Days

  • 4–8 kg fat loss
  • Visible face fat reduction
  • 1–2 inch waist drop

Why Indian Grooms Gain Weight

  • Too many carbs (roti + rice + sugar)
  • Late dinners
  • No protein
  • Too much sitting

Phase 1 (Day 1–20)

  • No maida, sugar, alcohol
  • 3–4L water daily
  • 30 min walk
  • Protein breakfast

Phase 2 (Day 21–45)

  • High protein every meal
  • Gym 5 days/week
  • Controlled carbs

Phase 3 (Day 46–60)

  • Low sodium
  • No alcohol
  • 10k steps daily
  • Good sleep

Biggest Mistakes

  • Crash dieting
  • No protein
  • Only cardio
  • Late eating
