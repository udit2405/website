Workout Plan Before Wedding for Indian Grooms

If your wedding is in the next 2–4 months and you're still unsure what to do in the gym, this is your blueprint.

A proper workout plan before wedding isn't about exhausting yourself every day. It's about training smart — targeting the right muscle groups, managing fatigue, and building a physique that looks exceptional in every outfit.

What Should a Pre-Wedding Workout Plan Achieve?

A great workout plan before wedding should do three things:

  • Burn body fat
  • Build visible muscle tone
  • Improve posture and presence

Common Mistakes Grooms Make

  • Training only chest and biceps
  • Too much cardio
  • No structured plan
  • Ignoring diet
  • Burning out

The 5-Day Workout Plan Before Wedding

  • Day 1 — Push
  • Day 2 — Pull
  • Day 3 — Cardio
  • Day 4 — Legs
  • Day 5 — Full Body
